Clean your muscles. Toast some garlic in olive oil over high heat. Add your muscles and season liberally wit salt an pepper then add a glass of white wine to the pan. Invert another pan over top and steam muscles open for about 5 minutes. Throw out any that don't open. Toss in shelled edamame heavy cream and finely diced peppers in adobo sauce. Allow the sauce to reduce and serve over toasted crusty bread.
